关于 YU TATTOO

About YU TATTOO

YU TATTOO专注手针纹身艺术。手针纹身，摒弃机械冰冷，以手工之温，逐针雕琢灵魂印记。灵感源自万物，凭精湛手技，细腻针法绘就多样质感。从精致纹理至鲜明色彩，皆精准呈现。

YU TATTOO specializes in hand-poked tattoo art. Moving away from mechanical coldness, we create soul-deep impressions with the warmth of handcraft. Drawing inspiration from all things, our masterful technique and delicate needle work create diverse textures. From intricate patterns to vivid colors, every detail is precisely rendered.

严守卫生高标，用优质材具，以专业素养，倾心聆听每位顾客心声，为其定制独属的肌肤故事。于我而言，手针纹身不只是技艺，更是情感与信念的艺术化表达，愿以匠心，为每一寸肌肤注入永恒魅力。

We maintain the highest hygiene standards and use premium materials. With professional dedication, we listen attentively to each client's vision, creating personalized skin stories. To us, hand-poked tattooing is more than technique—it's an artistic expression of emotion and belief. With craftsmanship, we infuse eternal charm into every inch of skin.
